Safety Box Instructions

1
To Lock: Choose a personal code (4-6 digits), enter it, and press the # key.
2
To Open: Enter your personal code and press the # key.
3
Ensure the door is properly closed after locking.
4
If a wrong code is entered multiple times, the safe will temporarily lock for security.

Important Notices

- Please do not leave the safety box door open.

- The hotel is not responsible for valuables left outside the safety box.

- In case of any issues, please contact the hotel reception for assistance.
